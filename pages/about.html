About Us

About LotoPowerball

An independent source for US Powerball results, jackpot tracking, and information on where to play online. We're not affiliated with the Multi-State Lottery Association or any official lottery operator.

Mission

What we do

We publish accurate, timely information about US Powerball drawings and connect players with reputable platforms for buying tickets online. Our goal is simple: give people who care about Powerball a clean, fast, trustworthy place to check results and learn how to play — without the visual clutter or aggressive ads that dominate most lottery websites.

We focus on Powerball specifically because it's the world's most-played lottery, with jackpots that regularly cross half a billion dollars. Coverage of other games may follow.

Standards

Editorial standards

The data on this site comes from publicly available sources, primarily the New York State Gaming Commission's open data feed and verified secondary sources for jackpot amounts. Results are typically updated within minutes of each drawing.

When we review or recommend online lottery platforms, we evaluate them on these criteria:

  • Licensing. Operating with a license from a recognized gambling regulator
  • Operating history. Years in business, public record of paying winners
  • Customer protection. Secure payments, fair dispute resolution, clear terms
  • Transparency. Clear explanation of how tickets are bought or bets are placed
  • Player feedback. Independent reviews and reputation across multiple sources

We do not list platforms we wouldn't use ourselves.

Money

How we make money

LotoPowerball is supported by affiliate commissions and advertising. When you click a link to a partner platform and sign up or buy tickets, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This is the same model used by most product review sites.

We don't accept payment for positive reviews. Our affiliate partnerships do not influence which platforms we recommend or how we rank them — we recommend services we believe genuinely serve players well. Full details are on our Affiliate Disclosure page.

Independence

Who we are not

LotoPowerball is an independent publisher. We are not affiliated with:

  • The Multi-State Lottery Association (MUSL), which operates Powerball
  • Any US state lottery or state government agency
  • The Powerball brand, trademark holders, or game operators
  • Any single ticket retailer, courier, or lottery betting platform

We exist outside the official lottery infrastructure as a third-party source of information.
