Get in Touch

Contact us

We respond to most inquiries within 2 business days. Choose the right address for what you need.

General

General inquiries

For most questions, comments, or general feedback:

contact@lotopowerball.com

Please include a clear subject line. We read every email but cannot always reply individually.

01

Corrections & data errors

If you spot incorrect results, wrong jackpot amounts, broken links, or any factual error on the site, please let us know immediately:

corrections@lotopowerball.com

Include the URL of the page and a brief description of the issue. We aim to fix errors within 24 hours.

02

Partnership & affiliate inquiries

If you operate a lottery platform, ticket courier, or related service and want to discuss inclusion on our site:

partners@lotopowerball.com

Please include your platform name, license/jurisdiction, and a brief description of your service. We evaluate partners against the criteria listed on our About page.

03

Press & media

Journalists and media outlets covering lottery topics:

press@lotopowerball.com

We're happy to provide data, commentary, or quotes on Powerball-related stories. Please include your outlet, deadline, and what kind of information you need.

04

Privacy & data requests

To request your personal data, opt out of tracking, or exercise rights under GDPR/CCPA:

privacy@lotopowerball.com

Include the email address used (if any) and a brief description of your request. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Note

What we can't help with

We cannot:

  • Verify whether a ticket has won — check with the issuing lottery or platform
  • Help claim prizes — contact the relevant state lottery or platform's support
  • Provide gambling advice or "picks" — we publish statistics, not predictions
  • Resolve disputes with third-party lottery platforms — contact them directly
Response Times
  • General — 2 business days
  • Corrections — 24 hours
  • Partnerships — 5 business days
  • Press — same day if possible
  • Privacy requests — 30 days max
